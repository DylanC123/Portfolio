Evolution Simulation - A level coursework mark 70/70

It involved the life cycle of Rabbits with basic attributes - eating, drinking and reproduction - which evolved over time. This is a very basic form of AI, representing 'Survival of the Fittest', showing a real life example of a genetic algorithm.
